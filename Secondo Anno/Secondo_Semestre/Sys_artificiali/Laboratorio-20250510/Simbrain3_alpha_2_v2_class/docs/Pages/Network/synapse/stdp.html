Spike Timing Dependent PlASTICITY

A variant on the Hebb rule for synapses between spiking neurons, where the relative timing of pre and post-synaptic spikes determines whether the synapse is strengthened or weakened.   When the pre-synaptic neurons spikes before the post-synaptic neuron, the weight is strengthened.  When the pre-synaptic spike occurs after the post-synaptic spike, the weight is weakened. 

Source: Thivierge, Scholarpedia article.
